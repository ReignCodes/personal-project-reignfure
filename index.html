The Fridge Project

At The Fridge Project we aim to make it easier for people to find the community resources available to them. Here we want to help build a community around mutual aid. A place where people can find and add places for people in need to find free food, meals, and other kinds of help.

Fridges & Pantries

Here we list local fridges and pantries as well as allow people to update when they were last filled/if they need food

Food Banks, Soup Kitchens, and Prepared Meals

Here we list local places that provide home made meals for take away or dining on site as well as local food banks

Warming Centers and Shelters

Here we provide locations, contact info, and other details for local warming centers and shelters

The Calendar

This calendar will be updated monthly and keep track of local mutual aid events, food pantry and soup kitchen dates and times, and other important mutual aid information
